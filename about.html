Who We Are

Stewards of Living Architecture

We are a landscape architecture and design company based in Georgia with 5 years of experience in creating high-quality outdoor environments.

We design and build spaces for private homes, villas, hotels, and large estates — with a clear focus on luxury, simplicity, and long-term durability.

Our team includes landscape architects, designers, and technical specialists working together to create landscapes that look good, function well, and feel naturally connected to their surroundings.

We believe in:

  • check_circle Modern, well-structured design
  • check_circle Durable materials and smart planning
  • check_circle Professional execution and reliable service

Who We Serve

Visionaries Seeking Sanctuary

We serve Tbilisi, Kakheti, Batumi, Bakuriani, Gudauri, and other residential and resort regions across Georgia — with complete attention to detail, privacy, and consistent quality.

  • check_circle Homeowners & Private Villas: Functional, low-maintenance, and beautifully organized outdoor spaces for private properties and vacation homes.
  • check_circle Hotels, Resorts & Estates: Well-designed outdoor environments that improve guest experience and increase the overall value of hospitality properties.
  • check_circle Country Homes & Large Estates: Landscape planning, pathways, lighting, structured gardens, and long-term care for large residential territories.

Our Philosophy

Rooted in Nature

We believe that the best landscapes are simple, beautiful, and close to nature.

Our work focuses on simplicity, creating outdoor spaces that feel natural, modern, and effortless.

Our goal is to design spaces that remain functional, refined, and beautiful through all four seasons, ensuring that every season brings new character without losing comfort or style.

How We Work

01

Discovery

We begin by listening to the land and to you. Soil analysis, site history, and your vision merge to form the brief.

02

Design

Our studio crafts detailed blueprints, 3D visualizations, and planting palettes that balance aesthetics with ecology.

03

Build

Precision construction. We work with master craftsmen to lay stone, install irrigation, and plant with care.

04

Sustain

A garden is never finished. We provide seasonal care plans to ensure your landscape matures beautifully.
