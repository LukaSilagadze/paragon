About Us

Our Philosophy

We believe that the best landscapes are simple, beautiful, and close to nature.

Our work focuses on simplicity, creating outdoor spaces that feel natural, modern, and effortless.

Our goal is to design spaces that remain functional, refined, and beautiful through all four seasons, ensuring that every season brings new character without losing comfort or style.

Studio Image

Who We Are

We are a landscape architecture and design company based in Georgia with 5 years of experience in creating high-quality outdoor environments.

We design and build spaces for private homes, villas, hotels, and large estates — with a clear focus on luxury, simplicity, and long-term durability.

Our team includes landscape architects, designers, and technical specialists working together to create landscapes that look good, function well, and feel naturally connected to their surroundings.

We believe in:

  • Modern, well-structured design
  • Durable materials and smart planning
  • Professional execution and reliable service

Who We Serve

We work with clients across Georgia who value quality, aesthetics, and professional service

Homeowners & Private Villas

Functional, low-maintenance, and beautifully organized outdoor spaces for private properties and vacation homes.

Hotels, Resorts & Estates

Country Homes & Large Estates

Our Services

Landscape Architecture & Design

We create clear, practical, and visually refined landscape designs tailored to your property.

Our process includes site analysis, layout planning, plant selection, zoning, lighting, and full hardscape coordination.

You receive a professional design plan that:

  • fits your lifestyle
  • matches your home's style
  • is easy to maintain
  • improves functionality and long-term value

Sod Installation

High-quality sod installation for an immediate green lawn.

We use premium sod, prepare the soil correctly, and ensure long-term health through professional maintenance options.

Lawn Care & Mowing

Full lawn management to keep your garden healthy and clean.

Services include mowing, fertilizing, irrigation checks, weed control, and pest management — keeping the lawn consistently green and well-maintained.

Irrigation System Installation

Automated irrigation systems designed for efficient water use and healthy plant growth.

We plan, install, and calibrate systems that ensure balanced watering with minimal maintenance.

Terrace Construction & Outdoor Grading

Custom terraces built to enhance outdoor living space.

We design and construct terraces that improve functionality, extend usable space, and match your home's architecture.

Garden Maintenance & Year-Round Care

Complete garden care throughout the year:

  • Weekly or monthly maintenance
  • Seasonal planting
  • Tree and topiary shaping
  • Soil & nutrition management
  • Pest and plant health control

Your garden remains organized, healthy, and attractive in all seasons.

Hardscaping

Custom stonework, pathways, patios, retaining walls, and decorative structures.

We use durable materials and clean, modern designs to create long-lasting and visually appealing hardscape features.

Decorative & Premium Features

Enhancing outdoor spaces with:

  • Custom decorative elements
