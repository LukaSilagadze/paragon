Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small garden refreshes may take 2-4 weeks, while complete backyard transformations typically require 8-16 weeks from design to completion.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your project vision, assess your space, and provide preliminary ideas. This helps us understand your needs before creating a detailed proposal.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Greenville metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. For projects outside our standard service area, we may charge a travel fee. Contact us to see if we serve your location.

Can you work with my budget?

Absolutely. We create designs that align with your budget and can phase projects to make them more manageable. During our consultation, we'll discuss budget options and find solutions that work for you.
