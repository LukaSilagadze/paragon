Services

We are a one-stop service provider that fully supports our satisfied clients from ideation to full implementation and aftercare.

Complete Lighting Design Services

We provide end-to-end lighting design services that transform spaces through thoughtful illumination. Our comprehensive approach ensures every project receives meticulous attention from initial consultation through final implementation.

Our team combines creative vision with technical expertise to deliver lighting solutions that enhance architecture, improve functionality, and create memorable experiences.

01

Conceptual Design & Site Assessment

We begin every project with a thorough understanding of your vision, site conditions, and design objectives.

Client Consultation

In-depth discussions to understand your vision, preferences, and functional requirements. We listen carefully to your needs and translate them into a comprehensive lighting strategy.

Site Analysis

Comprehensive evaluation of your space including architectural features, existing lighting conditions, environmental factors, and technical constraints to inform the design process.

Developing a Design Intent

Creating a clear design direction that aligns with your goals, architectural style, and functional needs. This foundational document guides all subsequent design decisions.

02

Design Development (Schematic Design)

Transforming concepts into detailed lighting plans that balance aesthetics, functionality, and technical requirements.

Lighting Plan

Detailed layouts showing fixture placement, beam angles, and coverage areas. Our plans ensure optimal illumination while maintaining visual harmony with the architecture.

Fixture Selection

Curated selection of lighting fixtures that match your aesthetic, meet performance requirements, and align with budget considerations. We specify products from trusted manufacturers.

Lighting Strategy

Comprehensive approach to lighting that addresses ambient, task, and accent lighting needs. We create layered lighting schemes that enhance both form and function.

03

Detailed Design & Documentation (Construction Documents)

Precise technical documentation that enables accurate implementation and ensures design integrity throughout construction.

Specifications

Comprehensive technical specifications for all lighting equipment, including performance requirements, finishes, and installation details to ensure quality and consistency.

Detailed Layouts

Precise drawings showing exact fixture locations, mounting details, and integration with architectural elements. These documents serve as the blueprint for installation.

Load Calculations

Technical analysis of electrical loads, circuit requirements, and power distribution to ensure safe, efficient, and code-compliant electrical systems.

Coordination

Collaboration with architects, engineers, and contractors to integrate lighting seamlessly with other building systems and ensure smooth project execution.

04

Implementation and Project Management

Ongoing support throughout construction to ensure your lighting design is implemented exactly as envisioned.

Contractor Liaison

Direct communication with installation teams to clarify design intent, answer questions, and resolve technical issues that arise during construction.

Site Monitoring

Regular site visits to verify installation progress, ensure quality standards are met, and address any deviations from the design before they become costly problems.

Shop Drawing Review

Thorough review of contractor submittals to verify that proposed fixtures, mounting details, and installation methods align with design specifications and quality standards.
